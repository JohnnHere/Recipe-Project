Peanut Fit Shake

This shake is an awesome choice if you want an energizer to start your day.
It will make you satisfied and in a good mood to work, study or exercize.
Ingredients:

  • 300ml of lowfat milk
  • 100g of banana
  • 40g of peanut cream
  • 20g of whey protein

Just mix everything at once. Its better and cleaner if you use a mixer tool. Drink it cold and have fun.
